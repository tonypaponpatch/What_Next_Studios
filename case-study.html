Overview

A luxury brand that needed a
website to match.

Aurum Collective is a Bangkok-based jewellery label targeting the premium lifestyle market across Southeast Asia. They had a strong product and sophisticated aesthetic — but a website built on a generic template that undermined the brand at every click.

The brief was clear: build something that feels as refined as the jewellery itself. Editorial. Unhurried. Confident. And, crucially, capable of converting high-intent shoppers without ever feeling like a typical e-commerce site.

Web Design Development E-Commerce Brand Direction Copywriting Motion Design
The Challenge

A luxury product in a
discount-feeling
wrapper.

The existing site used a mass-market Shopify theme with stock imagery and generic typography. The brand's premium positioning was completely undercut by the digital experience — visitors weren't staying long enough to engage with the product, and the conversion rate reflected that.

The Solution

Editorial architecture,
e-commerce
precision.

We rebuilt from scratch with a custom layout system inspired by luxury print editorial — full-bleed photography, generous white space, and typography that slows the reader down. Underneath the beauty, every interaction was engineered to guide visitors toward purchase with minimal friction.
