Frequently Asked

Common
questions.

How long does a website project take? +

A landing page typically takes 1–2 weeks. A full brand website runs 3–6 weeks depending on scope and how quickly content is supplied. Complex e-commerce projects are scoped individually.

Do you work with clients outside the US? +

Absolutely. The studio was built for international work — we collaborate with clients in Thailand, Southeast Asia, and across the US. All communication is available in English and Thai.

What do you need from me to get started? +

Usually just a brief conversation and any existing brand assets you have. We'll guide you through the rest with a structured intake questionnaire once the project is confirmed.

Do you offer payment plans? +

Yes. Most projects are split into a 50% deposit to begin and 50% on completion. For larger projects we can arrange milestone-based payments — just ask.

Will I own everything when the project is done? +

Yes. All final files, source files, and IP transfer to you upon final payment. We never hold projects hostage or require ongoing platform fees.
